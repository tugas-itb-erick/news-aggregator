Welcome to The Hottest News Aggregator in The World!


Borborygmutty is a news aggregator that uses string matching algorithm for searching news from RSS feeds. The string matching algorithm can be divided into three types such as Boyer Moore, Knuth-Morris-Pratt, and Regular Expression.
